HOW IT WORKS

1. Order a Pet Safety QR tag and attach it to your pet's collar.
2. Create an online profile for your pet with a photo, medical information, and your contact details.
3. If your pet goes missing, anyone who finds them can scan the tag with any smartphone camera — no app needed.
4. You receive an instant alert with the finder's GPS location, so you can reunite with your pet quickly.
